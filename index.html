HONEST YOGA DAY CERTIFICATE
The One Nobody Is Giving Out

This certificate is proudly awarded to

Your Name

in wholehearted recognition of their exemplary commitment to the International Day of Yoga — demonstrated faithfully, every year since its inception in 2015, entirely without the inconvenience of actual practice.

The undersigned confirms that the above-named participant has not observed Yama, Niyama, Pratyahara, Dharana, Dhyana, or Samadhi in any year under review, and does not intend to. They do, however, occasionally attempt Asana and Pranayama — the two limbs most amenable to photography — and consider this a complete and sufficient Yoga practice. They have dressed impeccably in the official Yoga Day T-shirt every year, contributed generously to plastic waste and post-event litter before, during, and after each celebration — confirming that Saucha, the first Niyama, remains across a full decade an aspirational concept — and have posted no fewer than four photographs per year on social media, each carefully composed to conceal the surrounding litter. No measurable change in physical, mental, or ecological well-being has been recorded across any of the years in question.

Nevertheless — the T-shirt was worn. The mat was unrolled for the photograph. The certificate was downloaded. And that, in the spirit of our times, is more than enough.
