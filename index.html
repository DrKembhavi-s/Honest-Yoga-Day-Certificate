On the Ashtanga of Yoga — Patanjali Yoga Sutras 2.29
  • ☑Has not observed Yama — practising neither Ahimsa, Satya, Asteya, Brahmacharya, nor Aparigraha in daily life; not even on Yoga Day
  • ☑Has not observed Niyama — Saucha, Santosha, Tapas, Svadhyaya, and Ishvara Pranidhana remain safely unvisited, today and every other day
  • ☑Has never attempted Asana beyond the posture required to hold a phone for a selfie
  • ☑Has not practised Pranayama, except the involuntary breathing that keeps them alive
  • ☑Has made no effort toward Pratyahara — the senses remain fully, enthusiastically, and gloriously outward

  • ☑Has never attempted Dhyana — meditation lasting more than three seconds has not been achieved
  • ☑Has attained no Samadhi — and does not personally know anyone who has
On the Spirit of the Celebration
  • ☑Has dressed impeccably in the official Yoga Day T-shirt, mat bag, and full paraphernalia — ensuring maximum visibility and minimum practice
  • ☑Has participated enthusiastically in generating plastic waste through event kits, bottled water, single-use carry bags, and promotional material — before, during, and after the celebration — demonstrating that Saucha (the first Niyama) remains an aspirational concept
